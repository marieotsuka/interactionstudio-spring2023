Readings

Readings will be dispersed throughout the semester to inform and contextualize our work with each project. Be prepared to respond, raise questions, and discuss the text during class by posting a comment or question on the relevant Slack channel.

Each student will be responsible for being a lead in the reading discussion: Sign Up here. This involves kicking-off the discussion with a brief summary of the reading, preparing at least three discussion questions, sharing any relevant works, and readiness to respond to your peers’ responses.
